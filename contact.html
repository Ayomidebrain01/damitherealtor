Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start buying a home with Dami?
Simply send us a message using the form above or call/WhatsApp Dami directly. She'll schedule a free consultation call to understand your needs, budget, and timeline — then get to work.
Does Dami work with out-of-state or international buyers?
Absolutely. Dami is a relocation specialist and works with clients from across the US and internationally. Virtual tours, remote signings, and full concierge support are all available.
What areas does Dami cover in Greater Houston?
Dami covers all of Greater Houston including River Oaks, Galleria, Memorial, The Heights, Katy, Sugar Land, The Woodlands, Pearland, Friendswood, and 20+ other neighbourhoods.
What's your commission structure?
Commission is discussed transparently during your initial consultation. As a buyer, Dami's services are typically free to you — her commission is paid by the seller. Contact us for seller-side details.
How quickly can Dami list my property?
Once you're ready, Dami can have your listing live on HAR.com and across social media within 48–72 hours — complete with professional photography and a full marketing campaign.
Does Dami assist with investment properties?
Yes — investment real estate is one of Dami's specialties. From single-family rentals to multi-units and fix-and-flip opportunities, she can help you identify, analyse, and acquire high-yield properties.
