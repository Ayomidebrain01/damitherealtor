About This Property

Welcome to The Galleria Grand — a rare offering in Houston's most coveted luxury corridor. This exceptional estate commands 6,200 square feet of masterfully designed living space, set on an expansive 0.42-acre lot in the heart of the Galleria-Uptown district.

Designed for the most discerning buyer, the home features soaring 12-foot ceilings, wide-plank white oak flooring throughout, and walls of floor-to-ceiling glass that flood every room with natural light. The chef's kitchen is anchored by a 14-foot island, Wolf and Sub-Zero appliances, and a butler's pantry that rivals most homes' full kitchens.

The primary suite is a retreat unto itself — a private sanctuary with a spa bathroom, dual walk-in closets, and a private terrace overlooking the pool. Five additional en-suite bedrooms provide effortless accommodation for family and guests.

Outside, the resort-style pool, outdoor kitchen, and covered loggia create the perfect environment for Houston's vibrant outdoor lifestyle. Walking distance to Houston's finest dining, the Galleria, and Memorial Park.
