Dami's Story

Built on Hustle, Driven by Results

Dami Olatunde arrived in Houston with a vision — to bridge the gap between ambition and homeownership for buyers who deserved more from their realtor. What started as a passion for helping families find their perfect home quickly became one of Houston's most recognisable real estate brands.

With a background in finance and an innate understanding of the Houston market, Dami quickly rose to the Top 1% of Houston real estate agents — a distinction he's maintained for multiple consecutive years. His approach is simple: deep market knowledge, relentless negotiation, and genuine care for every client he serves.

Today, Dami leads a growing team, manages a portfolio spanning luxury estates to investment properties, and maintains an engaged community of 86,500+ followers on Instagram who trust his for real estate education, market insights, and property inspiration.

Whether you're buying your first home in Sugar Land, selling a River Oaks estate, or building a rental portfolio across Greater Houston — Dami Olatunde is the partner who delivers.
